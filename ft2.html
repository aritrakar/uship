Check out how we function.

Step 1: Sign Up.

We securely store your data and respect your privacy.(Coming soon: Blockchain)

Step 2: Select your address and the delivery address.

We will connect you to one of our guys who will come and pick up your parcel.

Step 3: Your product is delivered.Checkmate.

After collecting the package, the ball is in the delivery company's court.

Behind the scenes:

1. Customer request is sent out to delivery boys of the preferred company.

2. Algorithm sends request to geographically nearby delivery boys.

3. A delivery boy who accepts the request will drop by to price and pick up the package.

4. Package is sent to company warehouse for delivery.
